Natural Disaster Prediction and Response

In order to forecast and track natural disasters like hurricanes, floods, and wildfires, artificial intelligence (AI) analyzes a variety of data sources, including satellite imagery and weather patterns. Planning for evacuation and catastrophe response is made possible as a result.
