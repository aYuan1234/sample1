Agriculture and Food

Food Quality Inspection

AI inspects and rates the quality of food goods using computer vision. In order to prevent food waste and guarantee that only high-quality items reach the market, cameras and sensors are used to detect flaws, inconsistencies, or pollutants. And people become more and more concerned about AI-related publications on food safety

Predictive Analytics(Crop Yield Forecasting)

AI Machine learning algorithms use past data and present circumstances to forecast crop yields, giving farmers the information they need to plan their harvesting and marketing tactics.

The device enlarges to reveal a tomato plant's yellow blossoms. After that, an AI algorithm was trained to analyze the plant photos and make an accurate estimate of the time it would take for the tomatoes to ripen, be harvested, packed, and delivered to the store. The tomatoes and the technology in question are owned by Nature Fresh Farms, a 20-year-old produce farm with 500 acres in Ohio and Ontario. According to Keith Bradley, head of IT at Nature Fresh, the sales team's work is made easier and profitability is immediately increased by knowing exactly how many tomatoes will be available in the future.
